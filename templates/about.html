About Us

TrackRat is a package tracking website that helps you keep track of your online orders and deliveries in Canada. Whether you are shopping from Amazon, eBay, Walmart or any other online retailer, TrackRat lets you easily track your packages from multiple carriers and get real-time updates on their status and location.
TrackRat was founded on March 27th, 2023 by a team of passionate online shoppers who wanted to create a simple and reliable way to track their packages. Our mission is to provide you with the best package tracking experience possible, so you can save time and hassle and enjoy your online shopping.

With TrackRat, you can:

  • Track your packages from over 100 carriers in Canada and worldwide
  • Get notified when your package is shipped, out for delivery or delivered
  • View detailed information about your package, such as weight, dimensions, delivery date and tracking history
  • Share your tracking number with others or access it from any device
  • Manage your tracking preferences and settings


TrackRat is non-profit, and therefore free to use for everybody. Just enter your tracking number and start tracking your package. You can also bookmark our website for easy access.

We hope you enjoy using TrackRat and find it useful for your online shopping needs. If you have any questions, feedback or suggestions, please feel free to contact us. We would love to hear from you!
